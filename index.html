Notes on
Stillness

In a world obsessed with speed, we cultivate the art of pause. NOTES ON is an exploration of high-altitude teas and the profound silence they carry.

Sourced directly from the cloud-veiled peaks of Alishan, our teas are born in the freezing mist at 1,600m above sea level. This harsh environment slows the leaf's growth, locking in a dense, creamy sweetness that cannot be rushed.

We believe brewing tea is not just a preparation, but a ritual of returning to oneself. Every leaf is a note; every steep is a breath.

Our Philosophy

  • Patience — Slow growth, hand-plucked.
  • Clarity — Transparent sourcing, pure taste.
  • Stillness — The quiet moment in your cup.
